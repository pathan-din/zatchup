About Us

Overview

ZatchUp is as a three-way bridge between Educational Institutions, its alumni and students.

By using this unique bridge, ZatchUp provides all educational institutions a unique opportunity to have one click reach to all its alumni and students, which further enables them to provide best in class education and facilities to its students

How it works

At ZatchUp we help educational institutions by

Building alumni networks and drawing a detailed alumni engagement plan for educational institutions (Schools, Colleges and Universities)

Raising Donations for educational institutions from alumni

Providing one click access to all current students

Making educational institutions go digital
